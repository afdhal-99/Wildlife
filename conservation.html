The Sri Lakan wildlife conservation department



The Department of Wildlife Conservation in Sri Lanka is a dedicated governmental body committed to preserving the rich biodiversity of the island nation. Established to safeguard and manage the diverse ecosystems, the department plays a pivotal role in the protection of endangered species, habitats, and the promotion of sustainable wildlife practices. Through rigorous research, monitoring, and community engagement, it strives to strike a balance between conservation and human activities. With a focus on fostering harmony between people and nature, the department is at the forefront of ensuring the longevity of Sri Lanka's unique and irreplaceable wildlife heritage.
