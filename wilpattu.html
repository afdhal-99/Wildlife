THE WILPATTU NATIONAL PARK





Explore Wilpattu, a pristine wildlife sanctuary in Sri Lanka, renowned for its diverse ecosystems and abundant wildlife. Spread across 1,317 square kilometers, Wilpattu National Park boasts lush landscapes, ancient reservoirs, and dense forests. The park's unique feature is its natural lakes, or "villus," providing a haven for wildlife, including leopards, elephants, sloth bears, and myriad bird species. Embark on thrilling safaris to witness these majestic creatures in their natural habitat. The park's untamed beauty, combined with its historical and cultural significance, offers a captivating adventure for nature enthusiasts and wildlife lovers seeking an authentic and immersive experience in Sri Lanka's wilderness.



Few facts about Wilpattu

1.Diverse Ecosystems: Yala: rich biodiversity, from jungles to coastal lagoons.

2.Leopard Capital: Prime spot globally, Block 1 for thrilling leopard sightings.

3.Birdwatcher's Paradise: 200+ species, including vibrant junglefowl and migratory birds.

4.Historical Charm: Ancient rock inscriptions, remnants weave history into natural beauty.

5.Varied Landscapes: Beaches, lakes, plains create habitats for diverse wildlife.

Your experience at Wilpattu

1. **Oldest Park:** Sri Lanka's largest, oldest park spans 1,317 sq km. 2. **Diverse Ecosystems:** Varied habitats, from forests to lakes, support diverse wildlife. 3. **Leopard Haven:** Significant leopard population, ideal for leopard sightings. 4. **Birdwatcher's Paradise:** Over 200 bird species, resident and migratory, thrive here. 5. **Historical Charm:** Ancient ruins add historical and cultural significance to the park.

Precautions to take

1.Wildlife Caution: - Maintain safe distances; follow guides and park rules for wildlife viewing.

2.Responsible Tourism: - Avoid littering, noise; respect rules for minimal environmental impact.

3.Weather Preparedness: - Pack for heat, rain; stay hydrated during safari tours.

4.Permit and Booking: - Secure permits, bookings for safari tours; plan in advance.

5.Optimal Timing: - Visit during less crowded times for a serene wildlife experience.
